Five Best Strategies to Manage and Optimize Cloud Cost

Size Efficiently

to Reduce Cloud Costs

A major part of the Snowflake bill consists of compute costs. Scale and optimize your cloud compute resources to limit the cost per workload. Turn on or off the warehouse for certain workloads or when not in use.

01

step

Set up

Resource Monitors

Set up resource monitors for raising alerts and taking action upon reaching the monthly credits consumption threshold. This way, you will not exceed the monthly threshold or quota that you want to spend for compute on Snowflake.

02

step

Use Zero-copy

Cloning Effectively

Cloning is another unique Snowflake feature that can be tapped effectively. It helps optimize snowflake costs and reduce the execution time of the queries to improve both the user and customer experience.

03

step

Suspend Virtual

Warehouse When not in Use

We should not leave virtual warehouses to run idle when there are no queries to execute. It is best to auto-suspend them after certain minutes of idle time.

04

step

Analyze

Account Usage

Monitor account usage, especially query history and warehouse metering history. This helps track credit consumption and finds out workloads that took maximum time over a period and eliminate them.

step
